Adaptive Scenario-Based Assessment Prototype

Scenario 1: Alien Invasion Negotiation

Aliens arrive on Earth and are skeptical about Earth's cuisine. They ask: "Earth cuisine seems underwhelming. Some planets offer edible crystals and glowing beverages. What culinary advantage could Earth possibly have?"









Scenario 2: The Cheesy Startup

A potential large client worries about freshness of your online exotic cheeses: "Why risk buying cheeses online instead of from established local shops?"









Scenario 3: Celebrity Pet Influencer Agency

MegaMilk Dairy doubts your influencer-cat will meet compliance:"Cats are cute, but can they remain compliant with strict dairy-marketing rules?"









Scenario 4: Sherlock For Hire

A formal client doubts your detective style fits their structured environment: "We value structured methods—your style seems flashy."
