Adaptive Scenario-Based Assessment Prototype

Scenario 1: Alien Invasion Negotiation

Aliens arrive skeptical about Earth's cuisine. They say: "Earth cuisine seems underwhelming. Other planets offer edible crystals and glowing beverages. What culinary advantage could Earth possibly have?"









Scenario 2: The Cheesy Startup

Client says: "We're skeptical about freshness and delivery reliability compared to local cheese suppliers. Why risk buying cheeses online?"









Scenario 3: Celebrity Pet Influencer Agency

Client asks: "Can your influencer-cat clearly remain compliant with strict dairy industry marketing rules?"









Scenario 4: Sherlock For Hire

Client expresses concern: "Your detective approach seems flashy. We need someone structured and methodical. How do you explicitly fit our culture?"
